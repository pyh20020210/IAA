Anrealage Spring 2018: Paris Fashion Week

As we go through our daily routines, we are constantly putting tension on the clothes we wear. This tension then imbibes our clothes with a sort of physical power, which Morinaga has set out to visualize through light. Mechanochromic technology is used as a sensory tool in architectural structures to detect and visualize cracks ?sites of stress or tension ?on a building through a phenomenon which turns this tension into light.
